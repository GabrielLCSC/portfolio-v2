About me

As a student in the Coding and Digital Innovation program at IIM, I have always been passionate about this field.

With the goal of becoming a full-stack developer, I have put my skills to good use in several collaborative projects that have allowed me to evolve both independently and in a team.

For example, I had the opportunity to work with BRED Banque Populaire, where as a team, we had to propose a visual redesign of their mobile application.

As I'm still learning, my projects are few and far between, but I continue to evolve every day to propose more and more qualitative content.
